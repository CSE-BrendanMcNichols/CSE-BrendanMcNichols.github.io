Seattle Kraken

The Seattle Kraken is a professional ice hockey team based in Seattle, Washington. The team was founded in 2021 and is a member of the Pacific Division in the Western Conference of the National Hockey League (NHL). The Kraken's home games are played at the Climate Pledge Arena, in the heart of Seattle. The arrival of the Kraken in 2021 marked the return of major professional hockey to Seattle after nearly a century.

In their inaugural season, the Kraken faced the challenges expected of a new team, finishing near the bottom of the standings. However, the team showed significant improvement in their second season, which resulted in their first-ever playoff appearance. The Kraken made an impressive run in the playoffs, beating the reigning Stanley Cup Champions, the Colorado Avalanche, and nearly beating the Dallas Stars in a hard fought 7 game series in round 2, proving they could compete at a high level. Their success has fueled excitement among fans.
